Notes & substitutions

  • Glue sticks: the list asks for 8; the cheapest route is the up&up 2-count at $0.25 — buy four. Elmer’s sells a 6-pack ($2.99) for name-brand.
  • Highlighters: no 2-count assorted pack exists; the smallest is the up&up 4-pack ($1.99).
  • Pink erasers: sold in 3-counts (Paper Mate Pink Pearl, $1.49); grab two to exceed four.
  • 24-color tempera paint sticks: the only true 24-color set is KINGART ($19.99); Mondo Llama tops out at 18 colors ($10).
  • Washable finger paint (8-color): not stocked in that form; Crayola 10-count Washable Kids’ Paint ($6.69) is the nearest.
  • 15-gallon 24"×24" bags: not carried in that size; Hefty Jumbo storage bags ($4.39/pk) are closest, or check Ziploc Big Bags XL.
  • Store brands: many staples are cheapest as up&up / Mondo Llama / Dealworthy; swap to Crayola or Elmer’s if a teacher requires name brands.
